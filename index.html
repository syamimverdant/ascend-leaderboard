🔋 Battery Notes: HV vs LV

Full reference guide — scroll down for all sections

1. Function of Solar PV Batteries

What it does: Stores excess solar energy during the day for use at night, during peak hours, or during blackouts.

Why customers need it:

• Energy Independence — reduce TNB reliance

• Backup Power — lights, fridge, WiFi during blackouts

• Maximise Savings — use stored energy at full value instead of selling to grid at lower rate

• Peak Shaving — use battery during expensive hours

• Future-Proofing — as costs rise, stored energy = control

💡 Pitch: "A solar system without a battery is like a water tank without a tap at night."

How it works:

1. Daytime → panels power home → excess charges battery

2. Battery full → excess goes to grid (NEM)

3. Night → battery discharges to power home

4. Battery empty → draws from grid

5. Blackout → battery provides backup (if EPS configured)

The hybrid inverter manages all this automatically.

☀️ Solar PV Panels Notes

1. Function of Solar PV Panels

What it does: Converts sunlight directly into electricity. The primary energy-generating component of any solar system.

How it works:

1. Sunlight hits silicon cells → electrons knocked loose → DC electricity generated

2. Inverter converts DC → AC (usable power)

3. Home uses the power, excess charges battery or exports to grid

💡 Key Numbers:
• Peak sun hours (Malaysia): 3.4 hrs/day
• 10kW system → ~34 kWh/day
• Verdant uses 630Wp panels
• N-type panels: 30-year performance warranty

Daily Energy = System Size × 3.4 hrs × 85% efficiency

Example: 10kW → 10 × 3.4 × 0.85 = 28.9 kWh/day

🔌 EPS Box vs Critical Load Rewiring

Know The Breaker. Get It Right. Every Time.

THE ONE RULE

Check the MAIN INCOMING BREAKER rating at customer's house:

✅ 63A or below = USE EPS BOX (no change)

❌ Above 63A = DO CRITICAL LOAD REWIRING (no EPS Box)

NOT the inverter model. NOT the system size. Just the breaker.

WHY NOT EPS BOX FOR >63A?

• EPS Box max input = 63A per phase. House breaker 100A = can't handle it.

• Even if forced, you'd still rewire to stay within 32A. Box becomes useless.

Result: Skip the box. Just do rewiring. Same outcome.

3 STEPS

Step 1: Take CLEAR photo of main breaker (ampere number must be readable). Upload to SSR.

⚠️ Blurry photo = delivery chain stuck!

Step 2: Read the number:

• ≤63A → SSR = 'EPS BOX'. Done.

• >63A → SSR = 'Critical Load Rewiring'. Create ticket.

Step 3: Fill SSR correctly. Never write 'EPS BOX' if breaker >63A. Attach photo as proof.

COST — WHO PAYS?

Verdant covers rewiring up to RM1,500 or 32A. FREE for customer.

• Standard rewiring (up to 32A) = Verdant pays

• Customer wants MORE than 32A = top up at extra cost

• Additional points: RM200–350 per point

Be upfront: standard scope is covered. Beyond = their choice.

WHAT IS CRITICAL LOAD REWIRING?

Pick customer's essential loads (lights, fans, fridge, aircond) → wire into separate backup circuit → connected to inverter.

Max load = Inverter output × 80%
P3 Pro 30kW = 40A × 80% = 32A/phase = 7,360W max

⚠️ INDUCTIVE LOADS — startup draw 3–5×!
